Science & Technology Institutes in Maharashtra

Indian Institute of Technology Bombay (IIT Bombay)

Location: Powai, Mumbai

Established: 1958

IIT Bombay is one of the top engineering colleges in India.

It is known for engineering, science, design, and management courses.

Very competitive-top students in India go here

IIT Bombay is a premier engineering institution offering undergraduate to doctoral programs in science and technology.

Institute of Chemical Technology (ICT)

Location: Matunga, Mumbai

Established: 1933

It became an autonomous institution in 1994 and achieved deemed university status in 2008.

ICT is recognized as a Category 1 Institute by the Ministry of Education, Government of India.

ICT is known for excellence in chemical engineering, pharmacy, and biotechnology education and research.

College of Engineering Pune (COEP)

Location: Pune

Established: 1854

Location: Wellesley Road, Shivajinagar, Pune, Maharashtra 411005.

Accreditation: National Board of Accreditation (NBA).

Autonomy: Granted complete autonomy by the state government in 2004.

COEP is one of the oldest engineering colleges in Asia, offering programs in various branches of engineering and applied sciences.

Veermata Jijabai Technological Institute (VJTI)

Location: Matunga, Mumbai

Established: 1887

Established in 1887 as the Victoria Jubilee Technical Institute.

It is one of India's oldest engineering institutions.

VJTI is affiliated with the University of Mumbai and is financially supported by the Government of Maharashtra.

VJTI offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering and is known for its academic excellence.

Defence Institute of Advanced Technology (DIAT)

Location: Girinagar, Pune

Established: 1952

Location: Girinagar, Pune, Maharashtra.

Type: Deemed University under the Ministry of Defence, Government of India.

Chancellor: Shri Rajnath Singh, Hon'ble Raksha Mantri.

DIAT specializes in defense technologies and sciences under the Department of Defence Research and Development.

Vishwakarma Institute of Technology (VIT)

Location: Pune

Established: 1983

Vishwakarma Institute of Technology (VIT) is a prominent private engineering college situated in Pune

Established in 1983, it is managed by the Bansilal Ramnath Agarwal Charitable Trust (BRACT)

VIT is recognized for its academic excellence and infrastructure.

VIT is an autonomous engineering institute affiliated with Savitribai Phule Pune University, offering UG and PG degrees.
